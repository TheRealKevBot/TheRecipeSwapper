Who are we...?

We are a collective of culinary artists that believes through continuous collaboration there is eternal growth.

We are a collection of professional chefs working in kitchens throughout the world.

We are a collection of amatuer chefs aspiring to better themselves.

We are also a collection of mothers and fathers that want to provide our families with delicious healthy food
We hold the belief that anyone and everyone has the ability to make wonders happen within the kitchen. Whether it be within the most renowned kitchens in the greatest restaurant in the world, the small tight kitchens cramped within a food truck, or within the comfort of your own home. Cooking is something that is meant to be explored and experienced on your own. The recipes that are provided by our users are unique, in the fact that many were built from or rather off of other recipes of that same name. Here in lies one of the greatest wonders of cooking is that you can make it your own. Put your own spin on an old dish to transform it into something much, much more. This is meant to be a collaborative space where sharing you cooking experiences is one of joy and celebration. This is your chance to get your dish featured in a cookbook so to speak. This collection is meant to be an ever growing cookbook. A collection for the ages of anyone and everyone's dishes they wish to share with the world! We do not claim any ownership of the recipes listed within this collection. Sole ownership lies with the User who posted the recipe.
